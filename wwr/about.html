History

O.A.R started with a small group of river lovers who wanted to share the outdoors with others. We began guiding local families and students, focusing on safe and enjoyable rafting experiences.

Over time, we grew our routes, trained more guides, and improved our equipment. Today, we still keep the same mission: create safe, exciting trips that bring people together.
