About Outdoor Adventure Rafting

Outdoor Adventure Rafting (O.A.R) is all about safe, memorable river experiences. We guide beginners and experienced rafters through scenic routes and exciting rapids while keeping safety and teamwork as our top priorities.

Our guides are trained, our equipment is maintained, and our trips are designed to help you build confidence on the water. Whether you’re rafting with friends, classmates, or family, we’re here to help you have an amazing time.

History

O.A.R started with a small team of river lovers who wanted to share the outdoors with others. We began by guiding local groups and families, focusing on safety, clear instructions, and enjoyable trips for first-time rafters.

As more people joined our trips, we expanded our routes and improved our gear and training. Today, we continue that same mission: create adventures that are exciting, safe, and worth remembering.
